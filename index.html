⏱ Bio

Waffles is a 3-year-old tuxedo cat with a big personality and even bigger dreams. Adopted by Luffy after a fateful encounter at the Mission Bay waterfront, Waffles spends her days plotting world domination, knocking things off counters, and demanding ear scratches at 3am. Always dressed for the occasion in her signature black and white, she's the classiest cat in all of SF — and she knows it.
